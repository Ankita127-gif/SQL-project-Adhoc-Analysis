# 📊 AtliQ Hardware – Ad Hoc SQL Analysis Project

---

## 🔎 Project Overview

This project demonstrates **Ad Hoc Data Analysis using SQL** to solve real-world business problems.  
It includes database creation, structured queries, aggregations, filtering, joins, and insight generation to support **data-driven decision-making**.

🔗 Project Reference:  
https://codebasics.io/challenges/codebasics-resume-project-challenge/7

---

## 🏢 Business Context

AtliQ Hardware is a leading computer hardware manufacturer operating across:

- 💻 PC Division  
- 🌐 Networking & Storage (N&S)  
- 🎧 Peripherals & Accessories (P&A)  
- 🌍 4 Major Global Regions  

The management team required actionable insights to improve sales strategy, product expansion, and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The analysis was conducted to:

- 📌 Understand market penetration in the APAC region  
- 📌 Track product innovation and portfolio expansion  
- 📌 Optimize manufacturing costs and pricing strategies  
- 📌 Analyze sales seasonality and channel performance  
- 📌 Identify top-performing products and customer segments  

---

## 🗄️ Database Design

⭐ **Star Schema Database (5 Tables)**

### 🔹 Dimension Tables
- `dim_customer`
- `dim_product`

### 🔹 Fact Tables
- `fact_sales_monthly`
- `fact_gross_price`
- `fact_manufacturing_cost`

## 📈 Key Findings

- 🌏 **Market Coverage:** Atliq Exclusive operates in 8 APAC markets  
- 🚀 **Product Innovation:** 89 new products launched (245 → 334, +36.33%)  
- 🏆 **Top Segment:** Notebook leads with 129 products  
- 📊 **Growth Leader:** Accessories segment (+34 products)  
- 💲 **Manufacturing Cost Range:** $0.89 to $240.54  
- 📅 **Seasonal Peak:** Q4 2020 recorded 8.4M units sold (40% of annual volume)  
- 🛒 **Channel Contribution:** Retailers contribute 73.2% of gross sales  
- 💾 **Top Performer:** Networking & Storage Division (Pen Drive – 701K units sold)
